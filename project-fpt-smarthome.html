Project Overview

At FPT Smarthome Systems (previously with Rogo Solutions), I contributed to the development of embedded and mobile software for smart IoT devices. My work focused on integrating hardware components with cloud-based systems to deliver real-time monitoring and control functionality.

The project involved creating a comprehensive ecosystem of connected home devices that could seamlessly work together, be controlled through a single mobile application, and provide meaningful insights to users about their home environment and energy usage.
