Project Overview

Onibiocare is a healthcare startup brand offering home therapy services with innovative technology. Our slogan, "Vì bạn xứng đáng với sự chăm sóc tốt nhất!" ("Because you deserve the best care!") encapsulates our mission to provide top-quality healthcare services directly to patients' homes.

As a co-founder, I led the technical team in developing a comprehensive healthcare booking system that included a website and mobile applications, managing a team of over 15 members to create a seamless patient experience.
